The IAPP certification for privacy professionals and program managers. 

>[!Info] What is Privacy Program Management?
> The structured approach of combining several disciplines into a framework that allows an organisation to meet legal compliance requirements and the expectations of business clients or customers while reducing the risk of a data breach.

---
## [[Introduction to Privacy Program Management]]

Privacy program managers are responsible for the safekeeping and responsible use of personal information, they should be ready to demonstrate compliance with applicable data privacy laws, reduce risk and build trust.
