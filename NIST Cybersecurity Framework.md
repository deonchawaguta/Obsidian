# Introduction

- The NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) helps organizations manage and reduce their risks as they start or improve their cybersecurity program. 
- The CSF outlines specific outcomes that can achieved to address risk. \

>[!Info] The CSF is made up of the following components: 
>- [[CSF Core]]. A taxonomy of high-level cybersecurity outcomes that can help any organization's current and/or target cybersecurity risks. 
>- [[CSF Organizational Profiles]]. A mechanism for describing an organization's current and/or target cybersecurity posture in terms of the CSF Core's outcomes. 
>- [[CSF Tiers]]
