## Monitor
- To ensure critical systems are managed properly, the privacy function must rely on the information security function to ensure ongoing CIA of information, systems and applications 
- Documenting the privacy program baseline helps in identifying the systems that an organization uses

### Approaches to monitoring environment changes
- Develop policies for the change to IT systems and applications that include privacy protection procedures and assessments 
- Identify employees responsible for authorizing changes to the IT environment and deliver targeted training 
- Add procedures to ensure that new information systems and applications are not implemented without due diligence 
- Connect with IT service and product management functions across the organization, these functions have detailed change management procedures that should include privacy assessment 

####  Monitoring Employee Compliance with Privacy Policies 
- HR can monitor employee training and attendance 
- Compliance with policy can be included as part of performance reviews
- Technology tools can also monitor by scanning systems for confidential data

#### Monitor Regulatory Changes 
- Monitoring the regulatory environment begins with identifying the jurisdictions in which the organization operates 
- Common source s of regulation include:
	- National Legislation
	- Enforcement Agency Rules and Enforcement Actions
	- Judicial Decisions 
	- State Laws
	- Municipal Regulations
	- Self-Regulatory Systems

### Organizational Compliance Monitoring 
- Areas worth monitoring for compliance are as follows:
	- Privacy policy 
	- Records retention
	- Training and awareness 
	- Incident Response and Notification
	- Privacy Risk Assessment 
	- Internal Audit

## Audit 
- More formal tools for ensuring that a privacy program is properly maintained 

- *First-Party Audits* - Internal audits
- *Third-Party Audits* - External audits 
- *Second-Party Audits* - Audits on third party vendors, data processors or data sharing partners by the organization

- The output of an audit is a report that summarizes findings
