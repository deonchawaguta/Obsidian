- First appear in the U.S. Privacy Act
- Created to apply and adapt to many jurisdictions around the world

- There are 8 FIPPs:
	1. Transparency - Disclose how you handle PII 
	2. Individual Participation and Choice - Involve individuals in decision making
	3. Purpose Specification - Disclose the reason for collecting, using and sharing PII
	4. Data Minimisation - Only collect the PII you need a
	5. Use Limitation - Only use PII forwhat you have disclosed
	6. Data Quality and Integrity - Ensure collected PII is accurate and up-to-date
	7. Security - PII should be protected
	8. Accountability - Organisations should take responsibility for protecting data privacy in their operations
