# Introduction 

- IT Auditing in the examination and evaluation of an organization's information technology infrastructure, policies and operations.
- IT Auditors are specialized professionals who work with managements to enhance security, efficiency and compliance through practical recommendations

> IT Auditing encompasses various activities, including:
> - Evaluating security measures
> - Assessing system performance and reliability 
> - Ensuring compliance
