## Introduction
An ISC2 qualification aimed at information security and information assurance practitioners who works in Governance, Risk and Compliance roles and have a need to understand, apply and implement a risk management program for IT systems within an organization

## Domains

- Domain 1: Security and Privacy Governance, Risk Management, and Compliance Program
- Domain 2: Scope of the System
- Domain 3: Selection and Approval of Framework, Security and Privacy Controls
- Domain 4: Implementation of Security and Privacy Controls
- Domain 5: Assessment or Audit of Security and Privacy Controls
- Domain 6: Systems Compliance
- Domain 7: Compliance Maintenance
