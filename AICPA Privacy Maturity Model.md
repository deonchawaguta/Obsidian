# Introduction
- The AICPA/CICA Privacy Maturity Model is a model used to benchmark the progress of an organization's privacy program. The model is based [[Generally Accepted Privacy Principles|GAPP]] 
- The model is optimized for organizations that already have a privacy function or program in operation, a number of start-up activities are outlined for implementation before the model can eb used. 

>[!Note] Start-up Activities 
>- Identifying a project sponsor
>- Appointing a project lead 
>- Forming an oversight committee
>- Assembling a team to obtain and document information and perform an initial risk assessment
>- Managing the project by providing status reports
>- Providing a means to escalate identified compliance issues 
>- Identifying the desired maturity level by principle for the organization


The maturity of an organization's privacy program can be assessed when findings are documented and devaluated under each of the [[GAPP Privacy Criteria|73 GAPP Criteria]]


## The Five Levels of PMM

### Ad  Hoc
Procedures and processes are generally informal, incomplete and inconsistently applied

### Repeatable
Procedures of processes exist, however, they are not fully documented and do not cover all relevant aspects 

### Defined
Procedures and processes are fully documented and implemented and cover all relevant aspects 

### Managed 
Reviews are conducted to assess the effectiveness of controls in place 

### Optimised 
Regular review and feedback used to ensure continuous improvement toward optimisation of the given process
